# Abstract

> AI-powered credit scoring models used by digital lenders often function as black boxes - making high-stakes loan approval or rejection decisions without providing any interpretable reasoning to applicants, compliance teams, or regulators.
>
> Develop a system that makes AI-driven lending decisions explainable and auditable, enabling lenders to justify outcomes to regulators, detect model bias across demographic groups, and provide transparent reasoning to rejected applicants.
